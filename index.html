About Me

Hello there! I am a UBC 2023 graduate with my Bachelor's in Cognitive Systems: Computational Intelligence & Design. My current interests lie in user research and data visualizations, in particular the intersection between the two: how to make data accesible and understandable to diverse audiences. I am currently a UX design intern at Kindworks.AI assisting with UX architecture, engagement data, and social media content.
